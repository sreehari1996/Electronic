Company Overview & Industrial Services!

Earth Power Electricals LLP is a registered B-Class electrical contracting company providing comprehensive electrical installation, maintenance, and project execution services across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. Since 2012, we have been associated with respected public and private sector companies across Kerala, delivering reliable electrical contracting and trading services.
