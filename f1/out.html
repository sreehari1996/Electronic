Product Overview

The GPSMAP® 943xsv is a high-performance marine multifunction display featuring a 9” sunlight-readable screen, high-resolution CHIRP sonar, ClearVü and SideVü scanning sonar support, and built-in Garmin Navionics+ mapping for both U.S. and Canada. Integrated networking and wireless capabilities make it perfect for serious navigators and anglers.

  • Seamless chart integration with Navionics+
  • Network with radar, autopilot, cameras, and more
  • ActiveCaptain app support for smart notifications and trip planning
  • Rugged design built for marine environments
